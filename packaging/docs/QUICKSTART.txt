SPASynth Quickstart
===================

1. FIRST SOUND
   Load SPASynth in your DAW (or open the standalone app) and play. The
   Init patch is a single wavetable oscillator — instant sound, no setup.

2. BROWSE THE PRESETS
   Click the preset name at the top. A browser slides out with every
   factory preset, three per library pack:
     Keys     the SFX as a playable, keytracked instrument
     Texture  a granular cloud scrubbed by a slow LFO
     Pulse    a wavetable voice whose filter is pumped by the SFX itself
   Search, filter by pack or type, and star your favorites.

3. RANDOMIZE ALL
   The big accented button rolls a brand-new patch. WILD (next to it) sets how
   polite or unhinged the rolls are. Lock any section first (the LOCKS
   row) to keep what you like — lock FILTER and OSC, then roll just the
   modulation, for instance.

4. LOAD YOUR OWN SOUNDS
   Any oscillator slot can be a Sample or Granular player: choose the
   mode, hit LOAD, and drop in any WAV — from the Silverplatter library
   or your own recordings. Its amplitude and pitch followers appear as
   mod sources (SFX A/B/C) in the matrix: route "SFX A Amp" to filter
   cutoff and the sound plays the filter.

5. ORGANIC CHAOS
   The CHAOS section adds slow analog-style instability: pitch, phase,
   position, amp, saturation and distortion drift, each with its own
   toggle. DEPTH/RATE/MIX are mod destinations too — try an envelope
   opening the chaos as notes sustain.

6. MAKE IT MOVE
   Right-click any knob for MIDI Learn. The arpeggiator's CHANCE,
   STUTTER, JUMP and HUMAN knobs turn patterns into performances; GLIDE
   (top right) adds portamento — try Legato mode with a long time.

7. MAKE IT YOURS
   The split-color circle in the header opens the accent color picker.
   One color or two — your call.

Have fun. The RANDOMIZE ALL button is the tour guide.
